Who is in the class?

I thought it would be useful to take a look at who is taking this class. Its a QMSS class, but how many non-QMSS students are here?

program2.df <-as.data.frame.table(table(factor(responses[[2]])),stringsAsFactors=FALSE)
program2.df <- program2.df[-8,]

program2.df[6,"Program"] <- "QMSS 3rd Sem."


ggplot(data=program2.df, aes(x=Program, y=Freq, fill=Program), main="Class Make Up") + geom_bar(stat="identity")+guides(fill=FALSE)
